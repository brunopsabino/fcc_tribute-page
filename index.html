Ronaldinho "the Wizard" Gaucho

The man who changed modern football.

Ronaldinho celebrating one of his goals for Barcelona F.C.

Here's a time line of Ronaldinho Gaucho's life:

  • 1980: Born in Porto Alegre, Brazil.
  • 1998: Started playing as a professional player.
  • 2000: At age 20, he moved to Paris Saint-Germain in France.
  • 2002: Won the FIFA World Cup.
  • 2003: Signing for Barcelona.
  • 2004: In his second season with Barcelona, he won his first FIFA World Player of the Year Award.
  • 2005: He won the inaugural FIFPro World Player of the Year.
  • 2008: Join Italian Serie A giants Milan on a three-year contract.
  • 2011: Ronaldinho returns to Brazil and joined Flamengo.
  • 2012: Ronaldinho made a move to Atlético Mineiro, just four days after leaving Flamengo.
  • 2015: Signed an 18-month contract with Fluminense.
  • 2015: Ronaldinho confirmed his retirement from football.
